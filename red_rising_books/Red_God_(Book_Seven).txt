Red God will be the seventh book in the Red Rising Saga and conclusion to the series.  The book was announced by Pierce Brown during San Diego Comic-Con on July 22, 2022.[1]

In a January 2025 interview, Pierce Brown estimated an early 2026 to a summer 2026 release date for Red God.[2] He also said it would be the longest book in the series with the possibly the book is split into two.
